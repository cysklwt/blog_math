## 常用不等式
$$\mid \mid a\mid-\mid b\mid \mid\leq \mid a\pm b\mid\leq \mid a\mid+\mid b\mid$$

 $$\sqrt{ ab }\leq \frac{a+b}{2}\leq \sqrt{ \frac{a^2+b^2}{2} }$$

## 绝对值求导
$$
\mid x\mid=\sqrt{ x^2 }
$$
$$
\mid x\mid^{'}=\frac{2*x}{2\sqrt{ x^2 }}=\frac{x}{\sqrt{ x^2 }}
$$
## 立方和公式
$$
 a^3 + b^3 = (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) 
$$

$$
 a^3 - b^3 = (a - b)(a^2 + ab + b^2) 
$$
