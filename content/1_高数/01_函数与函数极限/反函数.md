## $y = \ln(x+\sqrt{ x^2+1 })$
反双曲正弦函数  奇函数
$$
y = \ln(x+\sqrt{ x^2+1 })
$$
![|200](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020240801105546.png)
反函数（双曲正弦）奇函数：
$$
y = \frac{1}{2}(e^x-e^{-x})
$$
![|200](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020240801105809.png#pic_center)
双曲余弦 (特殊的悬链线)
$$
y = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2}
$$
![|200](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020240801110112.png)

>[!note] 
> $$x\to_{0}时，\ln(x+\sqrt{ x^2+1 })\sim x$$
> $$[\ln(x+\sqrt{ x^2+1 })]'=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ x^2+1 }}$$
## 双曲正弦函数的反函数互相推导
$$
x = \frac{1}{2}(e^y - e^{-y})
$$
$$
x e^y = \frac{1}{2}(e^{2y} - 1)
$$
$$
e^{2y} - 2x e^y - 1 = 0
$$
$$
e^y = \frac{2x \pm \sqrt{(2x)^2 + 4}}{2}
$$
