## 空间曲面
曲面方程：$F(x,y,z)=0$
## 二次曲面
$$
椭球面：\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}+\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}+\dfrac{z^2}{c^2}=1。
$$
![Pasted image 20241109204950|183](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109204950.png)
$$
单叶双曲面：\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}+\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}-\dfrac{z^2}{c^2}=1
$$
![Pasted image 20241109205004|155](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109205004.png)
$$
双叶双曲面：\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}-\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}-\dfrac{z^2}{c^2}=1
$$
![Pasted image 20241109205015|179](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109205015.png)
$$
椭圆抛物面：\dfrac{x^2}{2p}+\dfrac{y^2}{2q}=z（p,q>0）。（常考旋转抛物面x^2+y^2=z）
$$
![Pasted image 20241109205050|177](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109205050.png)
$$
椭圆锥面：\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}+\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}=\dfrac{z^2}{c^2}。（常考旋转锥面z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}）
$$
![Pasted image 20241109205129|160](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109205129.png)
$$
双曲抛物面（马鞍面）：-\dfrac{x^2}{2p}+\dfrac{y^2}{2q}=z（p,q>0）
$$
![Pasted image 20241109205204|199](https://animed-blog.oss-cn-wuhan-lr.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020241109205204.png)
$$
z=xy
$$
