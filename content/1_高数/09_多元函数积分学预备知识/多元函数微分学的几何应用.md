## 空间曲线的切线与法平面
(1)用参数方程给出曲线：$\begin{cases}x=x(t),\\y=y(t),t\in I\\z=z(t),\end{cases}$

其中 $x(t),y(t),z(t)$ 在 $I$ 上可导，且三个导数不同时为 0，则曲线在 $P_0(x_0,y_0,z_0)$ 处的切向量

$\tau = ( x^{\prime }( t_0)$, $y^{\prime }( t_0)$, $z^{\prime }( t_0) )$ ;

切线方程：$\displaystyle\frac {x- x_0}{x^{\prime }( t_0) }= \frac {y- y_0}{y^{\prime }( t_0) }= \frac {z- z_0}{z^{\prime }( t_0) }$ .

法平面方程：$x^\prime ( t_0) ( x- x_0) + y^{\prime }( t_0) ( y- y_0) + z^{\prime }( t_0) ( z- z_0) = 0$ .

(2) 用方程组给出曲线 $:\begin{cases}F(x,y,z)=0,\\G(x,y,z)=0.\end{cases}$

当 $\displaystyle\partial(F,G){\partial(y,z)}=\begin{vmatrix}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}&\frac{\partial F}{\partial z}\\\frac{\partial G}{\partial y}&\frac{\partial G}{\partial z}\\\frac{\partial G}{\partial y}&\frac{\partial G}{\partial z}\end{vmatrix}\neq0$ 时，可确定 $\begin{cases}x=x,\\y=y(x),\\z=z(x).\end{cases}$

其在 $P_0(x_0,y_0,z_0)$ 处的切向量 $\tau=\begin{vmatrix}i&j&k\\F_x^{\prime}&F_y^{\prime}&F_z^{\prime}\\G_x^{\prime}&G_y^{\prime}&G_z^{\prime}\end{vmatrix}_{P_0}=(A,B,C).$

切线方程：$\displaystyle\frac{x-x_0}A=\frac{y-y_0}B=\frac{z-z_0}C.$

法平面方程：$A(x-x_0)+B(y-y_0)+C(z-z_0)=0.$
## 空间曲面的切平面与法线
1. 用隐式方程给出曲面：$F(x,y,z)=0$，其中 $F$ 的一阶偏导数连续
$\begin{aligned}&\text{其在}P_{0}(x_{0} , y_{0} , z_{0} ) \text{处的法向量} \boldsymbol{n}=(F_{x}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}} , F_{y}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}} , F_{z}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}} ) .\\&\text{切平面方程:} F_{x}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}\cdot(x-x_{0})+F_{y}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}\cdot(y-y_{0})+F_{z}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}\cdot(z-z_{0})=0 .\\&\text{法线方程:}\frac{x-x_{0}}{F_{x}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F_{y}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F_{z}^{\prime}\Big|_{P_{0}}} .\end{aligned}$
2. 用显示方程给出曲面 $z=f(x,y)\Rightarrow f(x,y)-z=0$，其中 $f$ 的一阶偏导数连续
$\text{其在}P_0(x_0,y_0,z_0)\text{处的法向量}n=(f_x^{\prime}(x_0,y_0),f_y^{\prime}(x_0,y_0),\underline{-1})$
此法向量方向向下.
切平面方程：$f_x^\prime ( x_0, y_0) ( x- x_0) + f_y^{\prime }( x_0, y_0) ( y- y_0) - ( z- z_0) = 0$ .
法线方程：$\displaystyle\frac {x-x_{0}}{f_x^{\prime }( x_0, y_0) }= \frac {y- y_0}{f_y^{\prime }( x_0, y_0) }= \frac {z- z_0}{- 1}$ .
