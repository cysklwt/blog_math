## 一投二代三计算
设 $\Gamma:\begin{cases}x=x(t),\\y=y(t),\:t:\:\alpha\to\beta,\text{则有}\\z=z(t),\end{cases}$
$$\int_\Gamma P\mathrm{d}x+Q\mathrm{d}y+R\mathrm{d}z$$
$$=\int_{\alpha}^{\beta}\{P[x(t),\:y(t),\:z(t)]x'(t)+Q[x(t),\:y(t),\:z(t)]y'(t)+R[x(t),\:y(t),\:z(t)]z'(t)\}\:\mathrm{d}t\:.$$ 
## 斯托克斯公式
- 方向与 $\sum$ 的法向量成右手系
$$\begin{aligned}&\oint_{\Gamma}P\mathrm{d}x+Q\mathrm{d}y+R\mathrm{d}z=\iint_{\Sigma}\begin{vmatrix}\mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z&\mathrm{d}z\mathrm{d}x&\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\\\frac{\partial}{\partial x}&\frac{\partial}{\partial y}&\frac{\partial}{\partial z}\\P&Q&R\end{vmatrix}\text{(此为第二型曲面积分形式)}\\&=\iint_{\Sigma}\begin{vmatrix}\cos\alpha&\cos\beta&\cos\gamma\\\frac{\partial}{\partial x}&\frac{\partial}{\partial y}&\frac{\partial}{\partial z}\\P&Q&R\end{vmatrix}\mathrm{d}S\text{(此为第一型曲面积分形式 }),\end{aligned}$$
$$\text{其中}n^\circ=(\cos\alpha , \cos\beta , \cos\gamma )\text{为} \Sigma\text{的单位外法向量} .$$
