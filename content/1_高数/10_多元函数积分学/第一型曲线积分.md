## 计算
### 平面
$$
\int _{L}f(x,y) \, dx =\int ^b_{a} f[x,y(x)]\sqrt{ 1+[y'(x)]^2 }\, dx 
$$
$$\int _{L}f(x,y) \, dx=\int_{\alpha}^{\beta}f[x(t), y(t)]\sqrt{[x^{\prime}(t)]^{2}+[y^{\prime}(t)]^{2}} \mathrm{d}t.$$
$$\int _{L}f(x,y) \, dx=\int_{\alpha}^{\rho}f[r(\theta)\cos\theta, r(\theta)\sin\theta]\sqrt[]{[r(\theta)]^{2}+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^{2}} \mathrm{d}\theta.$$
### 空间
$$\int _{L}f(x,y) \, dx==\int_{\alpha}^{\beta}f[x(t), y(t), z(t)]\sqrt{[x^{\prime}(t)]^{2}+[y^{\prime}(t)]^{2}+[z^{\prime}(t)]^{2}} \mathrm{d}t.$$

> $$

> $$
